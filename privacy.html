Privacy Policy — Toolbox - QR Generator

Version 1.0

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Toolbox - QR Generator ("we", "app") handles user information. By using the app you agree to the terms described here.

What data we collect

  • We do not collect personal data by default. The app runs locally in your device's WebView.
  • If you choose to share a QR or use a cloud upload (not included by default), that may involve external services — those services have their own privacy rules.

Ads and third-party services

We use Google AdMob to show ads. AdMob may collect non-personal data for ad targeting and reporting. For details, see Google's privacy policy: Google Privacy Policy.

We may also use analytics (optional) which collects anonymous usage data to improve the app.

Files & QR generation

QR generation is performed locally in the app/web page. Small files can be encoded into QR locally. We do not upload your files to our servers. If a file is too large we will ask you to upload it to a cloud service and use the resulting URL to make a QR (that upload is done by you via external services).

app-ads.txt

We support app-ads.txt. If you see an app-ads.txt file on our GitHub Pages, it is used to declare authorized sellers for ad inventory.
